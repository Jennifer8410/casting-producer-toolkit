# Pre-Interview Questionnaire (Casting)

## Basics
- Name:
- City/State:
- Age:
- Pronouns:
- Phone:
- Email:
- Availability window:
- Any travel constraints:

## Your story (the hook)
1) In 2–3 sentences, what’s your story right now?  
2) Why is now the moment to tell it?  
3) What do you want (or need) most right now?

## Stakes + conflict
4) What’s the biggest obstacle you’re facing?  
5) Who disagrees with you (or what’s the friction point)?  
6) What do you risk losing if this doesn’t work out?

## Character + POV
7) How would your best friend describe you?  
8) What’s your unpopular opinion about this topic?  
9) What are you willing to say on camera that most people won’t?

## Scene potential
10) What can we see you doing (daily life/work/family/passion)?  
11) Who are 2–3 key people in your world we could meet?  
12) What would a “big moment” look like in the next 30–60 days?

## On-camera comfort
13) Have you ever been filmed/interviewed before? If yes, where?  
14) Any topics you won’t discuss on camera?  
15) Anything else we should know to tell your story accurately?
